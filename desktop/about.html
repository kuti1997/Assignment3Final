Founded in 2001, Dr. Computer Shop has grown from a small PC repair business to one of the largest and fastest growing value-added-resellers of technology products in Canada. We service customers of all sizes and types from Vancouver to Halifax and everywhere in between. Our 3 retail locations in southern British Columbia have been providing exceptional products and services to our communities for the better part of 17 years. We are now proud to sell products in-store and are able to continue to provide value to our customers all across this great country of ours.
